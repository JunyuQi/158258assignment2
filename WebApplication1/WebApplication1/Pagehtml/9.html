Queue Up To Fetch Water

Problem Statement

There is n Individuals line up to 1 tap to draw water, section i The time it takes for an individual to fill a bucket is ti How do you arrange the order in which they draw water so that the sum of everyone's waiting time is minimized?

Input

The first line contains the integer n
The second line contains n integers, where i integers represent i The amount of time it takes for an individual to fill a bucket ti

Output

Outputs an integer representing the sum of the minimum wait times.

Sample Input

            7

            3 6 1 4 2 5 7

Sample Output

            56
