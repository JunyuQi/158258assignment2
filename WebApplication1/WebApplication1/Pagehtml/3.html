Linked List

Problem Statement

Implement a singly linked list, the linked list is initially empty, and supports three operations: insert a number into the linked list header;
Delete section k the number after the number of insertions;
In k inserts a number after the inserted number.

Input

The first line contains the integer M to indicate the number of operations.
Next M Lines, each of which contains one action command, which may be one of the following types:
H x indicates the insertion of a number x into the header of the linked list
D k, which means that kth is deleted the number after the number of insertions (when k is 0 , the head node is deleted)
I k x, which means at k inserts a number x after the inserted number (In this operation k are greater than 0）

Output

A total row outputs the entire linked list from beginning to end.

Sample Input

            10

            H 9

            I 1 1

            D 1

            D 0

            H 6

            I 3 6

            I 4 5

            I 4 5

            I 3 4

            D 6

Sample Output

            6 4 6 5
